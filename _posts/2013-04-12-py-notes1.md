##List Slicing `list[start:end:stride]`   
---
 
`l = [i ** 2 for i in range(1, 11)]`   
_#Should be \[1, 4, 9, 16, 25, 36, 49, 64, 81, 100]_   
`print l[2:9:2]`   
_#Should be \[9, 25, 49, 81]_   
`backwards = l[::-1]`   
_#Should be \[100, 81, 64, 49, 36, 25, 16, 9, 4, 1]_
